What We Offer

Everything Your Society Needs

A complete suite of tools designed specifically for housing society administration in Pakistan.

Interactive Society Map

Real-time color-coded plot visualization. Green for available, red for sold, yellow for under construction. Updated live from the database.

Installment Tracking

Submit and track all payment installments digitally. Admins approve or flag transactions. Full payment history always accessible.

Role-Based Access

Separate dashboards for Admins and Residents. Every user sees only what they're authorized for — no data leaks, no confusion.

Maintenance Billing

Auto-generate monthly maintenance bills per plot and track paid vs unpaid status across all service categories.

Member Management

Complete resident registry with CNIC, contact info, booking history and plot assignments — all in one searchable database.

Audit Logs

Every admin action is logged with timestamp, old value, and new value. Full accountability trail for INSERT, UPDATE, and DELETE events.

About EstateSync

Built for Pakistani Housing Societies

EstateSync was designed to solve the real administrative challenges faced by housing societies — from tracking hundreds of plots to managing installment payments and maintenance bills. It replaces paper-based chaos with a clean, secure digital system.

11-Table Relational Database

Purpose-built MySQL schema covering Blocks, Plots, Members, Bookings, Payments, Staff, Services and more with full referential integrity.

Three-Tier Architecture

Clean separation of presentation (HTML/Bootstrap), application logic (Flask/Python), and data (MySQL) layers following academic best practices.

Secure Session Management

Flask sessions with RBAC ensure Admins and Residents are always routed to the right place with the right permissions.

How EstateSync Works

Get your society digitized in four straightforward steps.

1
Create Account

Register as a Resident or Admin. Your role determines which features and data you can access inside the portal.

2
Browse the Map

Explore the interactive society map. See available plots in real-time, color-coded by current status directly from the database.

3
Book & Pay

Select a plot, choose an installment plan, and submit payments digitally. All transactions are stored with receipt numbers.

4
Admin Approves

Admin reviews pending payments on the dashboard and approves them with a single click. Status updates instantly across the platform.
